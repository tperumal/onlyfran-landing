FRAN

Your life, organized

A brutalist life management app. Nine modules grouped into Daily, Growth, and Life — tasks, week, meals, fitness, career, goals, money, hobbies, weekends — and a dashboard that ties it all together. Dictate a brain dump and Claude routes it into the right places. No clutter, no decoration, just function.

How it works

01

Pin your most-used modules to the bottom nav. Swap them anytime from the menu.

02

Use voice commands to add tasks, groceries, bills, or workouts without touching the screen.

03

Everything syncs across devices via Supabase. Works as a PWA or native iOS app.
