FRAN

Your life, organized

A brutalist life management app. Eight modules — tasks, fitness, meals, career, money, hobbies, weekends, and a dashboard that ties it all together. Voice commands powered by AI. No clutter, no decoration, just function.

Open App Source

Dashboard

Everything at a glance

How it works

01

Pin your most-used modules to the bottom nav. Swap them anytime from the menu.

02

Use voice commands to add tasks, groceries, bills, or workouts without touching the screen.

03

Everything syncs across devices via Supabase. Works as a PWA or native iOS app.
